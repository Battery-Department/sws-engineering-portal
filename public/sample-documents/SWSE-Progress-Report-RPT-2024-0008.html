Work Completed This Period (1st - 15th May 2024)
  • Fire Tube Installation: Completed installation of all 24 new heritage-grade steel fire tubes with precision fitting and welding to original 1920s specifications.
  • Pressure Testing: Successfully pressure tested boiler to 270 PSI (1.5x working pressure) with zero leakage detected.
  • Non-Destructive Testing: Radiographic testing completed on all critical welds - 100% pass rate achieved.
  • Smokebox Preparation: Commenced restoration of smokebox assembly including cleaning, repair welding, and preparation for reassembly.
  • Quality Documentation: Updated all engineering drawings and prepared certification documentation for heritage railway compliance.
Issues & Risks
Minor Issue: Small amount of pitting discovered on original smokebox plate. Resolution: Additional cleaning and minor repair welding required (2 days additional work).
Risk Mitigation: Weather contingency plans in place for final outdoor testing phase. Indoor testing facilities secured as backup.
